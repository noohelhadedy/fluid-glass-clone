◆ ABOUT FLUID GLASS

We design and install high-performance glazing systems for ambitious architectural projects. Every pane reflects our commitment to clarity, quality, and collaboration.

Fluid Glass is a structural and architectural glazing company based in Watford, UK. We specialise in bespoke glazing solutions that bring architectural visions to life — from minimal frame sliding doors to expansive structural glass facades.
